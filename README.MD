## ğŸš€ Deploying Your First VPS

This tutorial will walk you through deploying your first VPS on the CarbonForge Discord server.

### Step 1: Join the CarbonForge Discord Server

Make sure you're in the CarbonForge Discord server where the bot is available. All commands must be used in that server.

### Step 2: Check Available Plans and Pricing

Before deploying, check what plans are available and their pricing. In Discord, type:

```
/price
```

Then select:
- **plan** - Choose from the dropdown (Nano, Starter, Builder, Advanced, Pro, etc.)
- **days** - Enter number of days (e.g., 30)

**What you'll see:**
- All available plans with resources (CPU, RAM, Disk)
- Price per day for each plan
- Total cost for the number of days you selected

**Example:**
```
/price plan:Starter - 6 CPU, 12GB RAM, 60GB Disk - $0.27/day days:30
```

This shows you'll pay $0.27 per day for 30 days = $8.10 total.

### Step 3: Deploy Your VPS

Once you've chosen a plan, deploy your VPS. In Discord, type:

```
/deploy
```

Then fill in:
- **name** - Choose a name for your container (e.g., "my-server", "web-app", "game-server")
- **plan** - Select the plan from the dropdown menu
- **days** - Enter number of days (1-365)

**Example:**
```
/deploy name:my-web-server plan:Starter - 6 CPU, 12GB RAM, 60GB Disk - $0.27/day days:30
```

### Step 4: Receive Invoice

After running `/deploy`, you'll receive:

1. **Invoice in Discord DM** - Check your Discord direct messages from the bot
2. **Invoice contains:**
   - Invoice ID (âš ï¸ **SAVE THIS!** You'll need it for payment)
   - Total amount due
   - Payment method (GitHub Sponsor, PayPal, etc.)
   - Payment link/instructions

### Step 5: Make Payment

1. **Use the payment method** specified in your invoice
2. **âš ï¸ CRITICAL:** Include your **Invoice ID** in the payment note/message
3. **Wait for confirmation** - The bot owner will confirm your payment

**Why Invoice ID is important:** Without it, the owner can't match your payment to your invoice!

### Step 6: Wait for Deployment

- Once payment is confirmed, your container deployment starts automatically
- You'll receive a notification in Discord when your VPS is ready
- This usually takes a few minutes

### Step 7: Access Your VPS

Once deployment is complete:

1. **View Your Dashboard** in Discord:
   ```
   /dashboard
   ```

2. **Your dashboard shows:**
   - Container name and ID
   - IP address
   - SSH port number
   - Complete SSH command to connect
   - SSH password
   - Resource usage (CPU, RAM, Disk)
   - Days remaining

3. **Connect via SSH:**
   - Copy the SSH command from `/dashboard`
   - Or manually connect: `ssh root@YOUR_IP -p YOUR_PORT`
   - Enter the password shown in `/dashboard`

**ğŸ‰ Congratulations! Your VPS is now ready to use!**

You can now:
- Install software
- Host websites
- Run applications
- Use it for development
- Do whatever you need!

### ğŸ® Container Management

#### `/power`
**Description:** Control your container's power state

**Usage:** `/power action:[start|stop|reboot]`

**Actions:**
- **start** - Start your stopped container
- **stop** - Gracefully stop your running container
- **reboot** - Restart your container

**Example:**
```
/power action:reboot
```

**Note:** Your container must be running to use `stop` or `reboot`. Use `start` if your container is stopped.

---

#### `/passwd`
**Description:** Change your container's SSH root password

**Usage:** `/passwd new_password:your_new_password`

**Example:**
```
/passwd new_password:MySecurePassword123!
```

**Important:**
- Choose a strong password
- Remember your new password - you'll need it to SSH into your container
- The password is changed immediately

---

#### `/pause` (Paid Plans Only)
**Description:** Pause your container to save resources (24-hour quota per plan)

**Usage:** `/pause`

**What happens:**
- Container is stopped
- Time is "frozen" - your expiry date doesn't advance
- You can resume anytime within your quota
- Quota resets when you extend your container

**Requirements:**
- Must have Advanced or Pro plan (or plans configured for pause)
- Must have at least 7 days remaining
- Must have pause quota available

**Note:** Pause quota is 24 hours total per plan. Once exhausted, container will auto-resume.

---

#### `/resume`
**Description:** Resume your paused container

**Usage:** `/resume`

**What happens:**
- Container starts again
- Time resumes counting
- Your expiry date continues from where it was paused

---

#### `/extend` (Paid Version)
**Description:** Extend your container's expiry time

**Usage:** `/extend days:30`

**Parameters:**
- **days** - Number of days to extend (1-365)

**What happens:**
- Creates an invoice for the extension
- You'll receive payment instructions via DM
- After payment confirmation, your expiry date is extended
- Pause quota resets when extended

**Example:**
```
/extend days:30
```

---

#### `/changeplan` (Paid Version)
**Description:** Upgrade or downgrade your container plan

**Usage:** `/changeplan plan:[select from list]`

**What happens:**
- Shows available plans with resources and pricing
- Calculates price difference for remaining days
- Creates an invoice
- After payment, container resources are updated

**Example:**
```
/changeplan plan:Pro - 16 CPU, 32GB RAM, 200GB Disk - $1.00/day
```

**Note:** Downgrades may require container restart. Upgrades are applied immediately.

---

#### `/price` (Paid Version)
**Description:** Calculate the price for a plan with specific days

**Usage:** `/price plan:[select plan] days:30`

**Example:**
```
/price plan:Starter - 6 CPU, 12GB RAM, 60GB Disk - $0.27/day days:30
```

**Shows:**
- Price per day
- Total price for specified days
- Payment methods available
